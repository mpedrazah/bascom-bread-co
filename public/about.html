About Bascom Bread

The Bascom Bread Co. is our family oriented, home bakery dedicated to crafting exceptional sourdough bread with simple recipes and natural ingredients. My name is Margaret, and together with my husband, John, and children, Dave and Joan, I am hoping to bring our love of fresh bread to our neighbors.

Using ingredients that are fresh and organic is important to me. I freshly mill my flour to ensure that my bread is more nutritious than your average loaf. The mix of fresh milled and regular flour results in a rich and hearty flavor and a soft, delicate texture.
